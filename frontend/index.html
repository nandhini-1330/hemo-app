About Anemia

Anemia occurs when you don't have enough healthy red blood cells to carry adequate oxygen to your body's tissues.

  • 💡 Low hemoglobin is the primary indicator
  • 💡 Common symptoms include fatigue & pallor
  • 💡 Iron-deficiency is the most common type
  • 💡 Early AI detection enables faster treatment

Visual AI Detection

Upload palm & eye photos for color-based anemia screening

💡
How it works: Anemia reduces hemoglobin, making palms and inner eyelids appear pale or white instead of healthy pink. The AI analyzes color patterns in your photos to detect these signs.
Tips for best results: Use bright natural light · Open palm fully · Pull lower eyelid down to expose the pink inner lining.

🖐️ Palm Analysis

Open your palm fully under good lighting. Pale or white creases between fingers are a key anemia indicator.
